Privacy Policy for Follower Analytics

At Follower Analytics, accessible from jacksebastian.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Follower Analytics and how we use it.

Introduction

At Follower Analytics, our main priority is the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the type of information that might be collected by Follower Analytics and how we use it.

What is Follower Analytics?

Follower Analytics is a locally used React application that interacts with the Instagram API to retrieve stats about a user's Instagram account. The primary motivation behind the creation of this app is to identify followers that the user follows but do not follow back.

Use of the Application

Follower Analytics is strictly a local tool, developed and used solely by its creator. It will not be hosted publicly or be available for public or commercial use.

Information We Collect

Follower Analytics might collect data provided by the Instagram API, such as follower details, based on user permissions. However, this data is purely for local analysis and is not stored, shared, or sold.

Why Do We Collect Data?

The data collected by Follower Analytics serves the sole purpose of analyzing follower relationships on Instagram, primarily to see which followers don't reciprocate a follow-back.

Local Usage Only

All data accessed and analyzed by Follower Analytics is only used locally on the device where the app is running. No data is transmitted, shared, or stored outside of this local environment.

Privacy Assurance

Given the local-only nature of Follower Analytics, user data remains private and secure. No third parties have access to the information the app might access or analyze.

Consent

By using Follower Analytics, you understand and consent to this Privacy Policy.
