Data Deletion Instructions for Follower Analytics

Thank you for using Follower Analytics. We prioritize your data's security and transparency.

Our Data Usage

Follower Analytics is a local-only tool, meaning all data interaction happens solely on your device. We do not have any server-side storage or persistent data storage mechanisms. Thus, there's no data of yours stored on our side.

Deleting Your Data

Since all interactions occur locally on your device, there's no data about you on our servers. If you wish to ensure that all data related to Follower Analytics is removed, simply uninstall or delete the local app from your device.
