About Me

I'm currently a junior at Texas A&M University at College Station double majoring in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics and graduating in triple honors: University Honors, Engineering Honors, and Mathematics Honors. I have a twin sister that also goes to Texas A&M with me, an older sister who graduated from the University of North Texas, and a beautiful girlfriend who is doing Pre-Med at the University of Texas at Austin.

I've competed in the Fall ICPC (International Competitive Programming Competition), TAMUhack 2020 (placed 7th overall at event), HowdyHack 2020, and TAMU Datathon 2020. These were exciting yet insightful experiences that taught me a lot about how much there is to programming and software development. During the Spring semester, I was invited to join TAMU Datathon on their Development team as a Tech Director.

Ambition and Competitive Nature

I was always a competitive kid growing up. I knew that as soon as got into high school and college, I would want to join a team or compete in something that would satisfy my crave to be the best. Once I found out about hackathons in freshman year, I got sucked in. I thought that the perfect way to showcase my drive and determination to be the best would be the compete in these hackathons that combined my creavitiy and programming skills.
