About Me

I graduated from Texas A&M University at College Station in 2023 with a double major in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics. I also obtained multiple awards and distinctions at graduation (Honors Fellow, University Research Scholar, College Honors). I have a twin sister that is in nursing school at Texas A&M, an older sister who graduated from the University of North Texas, and 2 dogs at home.

During my time in college, I was the President of a Google-affiliated organization called Google Developer Student Club. I was also the Vice President of a student-run organization called TAMU Datathon. We hosted an annual data science hackathon at the Memorial Student Center on campus and I have made so many lifelong friends from being a part of the organization. During the summer of 2022, I worked as a Software Development Engineer Intern within the Alexa organization.
